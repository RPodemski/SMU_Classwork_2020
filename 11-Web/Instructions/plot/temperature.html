Latitude vs Temperature

In the above figure, as we move closer to the equator the temperature rise, and as we move further the temperature is decreasing. If we compare the two hemispheres, we can see that the southern hemisphere's correlation with temperature is not as strong as the norhtern hemisphere. If we had pulled cities equally distributed between the two hemispheres, we might see similar correlations between the two hemispheres.
